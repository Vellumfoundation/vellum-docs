# Receive ETH

This page covers receiving ETH on Vellum.

## What you need

- A wallet connected to Vellum.
- Your Vellum address.

## Share your address

1. Open your wallet on Vellum.
2. Copy your address.
3. Share it with the sender.

The address format is the same as on Base or Ethereum, but make sure the sender knows the funds should be sent on Vellum specifically.

{% hint style="warning" %}
ETH on Vellum is a separate balance from ETH on Base or Ethereum. If a sender sends to your Vellum address from Base, it does not arrive on Vellum. They must be on Vellum, or use the bridge.
{% endhint %}

## Confirm receipt

- Your wallet should reflect the new balance once confirmed.
- The Vellum [block explorer](../network/block-explorer.md) shows the transaction by hash or address.

## Receiving via the bridge

If you are receiving ETH bridged from Base to Vellum:

- The bridge UI will show the deposit progress.
- ETH typically arrives within minutes after the Base-side deposit confirms.

See [Deposit ETH from Base](../bridge/deposit-eth-from-base.md).

## Privacy

Addresses on Vellum are public. Anyone can look up balances and transaction history by address on the block explorer. Do not assume privacy.
